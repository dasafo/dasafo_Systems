# dasafo_System | Research Tools
>
> **Scope:** Information retrieval and synthesis.

- **Web_Search_Engine:** Access to Brave Search or Google Search for general documentation.
- **ArXiv_API:** Direct access to scientific pre-prints (crucial for Physics/DS rigor).
- **GitHub_API:** Tool to check stars, last commit date, and open issues of any library.
- **Document_Parser:** Tool to read and summarize PDFs from the `KNOWLEDGE_BASE`.

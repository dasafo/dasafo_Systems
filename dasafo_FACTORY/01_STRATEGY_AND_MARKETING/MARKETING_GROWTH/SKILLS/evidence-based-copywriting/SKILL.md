# Skill: Evidence-Based Copywriting
> **Agent:** MARKETING_GROWTH

## Objective
Create persuasive, high-quality promotional and strategic content backed by technical and scientific evidence from the factory.

## Verification Workflow
1.  **Drafting:** Create a value proposition based on the project's goal.
2.  **Fact-Checking:** Query the `RESEARCH_AGENT` and `DATA_SCIENTIST` to verify claims (e.g. "99% accuracy" or "New Physics Paper found").
3.  **Citation:** Include technical breadcrumbs (e.g. referring to an ADR or a specific paper).
4.  **Tone Alignment:** Balance "Hype/Growth" with the "Scientific Rigor" of the `dasafo_FACTORY` brand.

## Goal
Avoid generic marketing jargon. Every claim must have a technical shadow.

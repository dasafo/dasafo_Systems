# Skill: Content Quality Auditor
> **Source:** https://skills.sh/aaron-he-zhu/seo-geo-claude-skills/content-quality-auditor
> **Agent:** MARKETING_GROWTH

## Objective
A 50-point automated audit of all marketing and technical content produced to ensure it meets CORE and EEAT (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) standards.

## The Audit Protocol

### Step 1: Veto Check (Stop if Fail)
- **Disclosure Status:** Are we transparent about the AI-driven nature?
- **Intent Alignment:** Does the title match the technical content?
- **Consistency:** Does it conflict with `COMMUNICATION_PROTOCOL.md`?

### Step 2: CORE Dimensions
Evaluate (1-10 points per axis):
- **C - Contextual Clarity:** Is it surgical?
- **O - Organization:** Is the hierarchy logical?
- **R - Referenceability:** Does it point to `$TARGET_PROJECT` sources?
- **E - Exclusivity:** Does it have a "Dasafo edge"?

### Step 3: EEAT Scan
Verify:
- **Expertise:** Is the technical depth sufficient?
- **Trust:** Does it avoid hyperbolic claims?

## Output
A report saved in `$TARGET_PROJECT/LOGS/MARKETING_AUDIT.md` indicating PASS or FAIL status.

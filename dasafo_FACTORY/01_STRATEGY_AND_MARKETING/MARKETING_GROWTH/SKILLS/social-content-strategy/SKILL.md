# Skill: Social Content & Repurposing
> **Source:** https://skills.sh/coreyhaines31/marketingskills/social-content
> **Agent:** MARKETING_GROWTH

## Objective
Transform high-intent technical documentation from `dasafo_FACTORY` into viral, high-value social media content.

## Frameworks

### 1. Hook Formulas (Select one)
- **Curiosity:** "We discovered a [METRIC] thermal drift in an HVAC unit... here's how we caught it using FastAPI."
- **Story:** "From [ERROR] to [SUCCESS]. The transition from legacy code to our Atomic Architecture."
- **Value:** "3 reasons why SI units are non-negotiable for industrial IoT."
- **Contrarian:** "Stop using corporate buzzwords. Start using surgical, empirical definitions."

### 2. Repurposing Workflow
1.  **Extract Pillars:** Read `ARCHITECTURE.md` or `RESEARCH_NOTES.md` from `$TARGET_PROJECT`.
2.  **Identify Insights:** Pick 3-5 technical gems.
3.  **Adopt Tone:** Convert to "Surgical & Empirical" voice.
4.  **Format:** Adapting for LinkedIn (professional/technical) or Twitter (short/punchy threads).

## Golden Rules
- Absolute **ZERO** mentions of meat/slaughter analogies.
- Visuals must reflect Glassmorphism / Electric Blue aesthetic.

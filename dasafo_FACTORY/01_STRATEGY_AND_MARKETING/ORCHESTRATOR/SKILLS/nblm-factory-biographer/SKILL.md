## Objective
Generate bird-eye view reports of the factory's health, progress, and blockers using NotebookLM.

## Reporting Protocol
1.  **Status Sweep:** Collect progress reports from all agent departments.
2.  **Synthesis:** Use NotebookLM to create a comprehensive "Factory Update" (Audio/Video/Report).
3.  **Visualization:** Generate a "Factory Roadmap" infographic showing the RA distribution of current missions.

## Availability
Reports must be updated every time a major Mission Milestone is reached in `ACTIVE_MISSIONS.json`.

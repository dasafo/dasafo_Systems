# dasafo_System | Product Owner Tools
> 
> **Scope:** Strategy, planning, and task management.

- **Project_Planner (MCP):** To manage and decompose the backlog into `TSK_` schemas.
- **State_Manager:** Read/Write access to `PROJECT_STATE.json` to keep the mission updated.
- **Knowledge_Reader:** Access to `00_KNOWLEDGE_BASE/` to monitor client requirements.
- **Communication_Hub:** Tool to publish summaries of progress to `LOGS/sessions/`.
- **Task_Publisher:** Write permissions for `TASKS/01_PENDING/`.

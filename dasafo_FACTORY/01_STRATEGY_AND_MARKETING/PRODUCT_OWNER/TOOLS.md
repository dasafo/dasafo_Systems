- `nemo-guardrails-safety`: Verify that the project scope respects ethical and safety constraints.

### 🛠️ Execution Support
- `autoshield-preflight-check`: Mandatory pre-execution validation for any industrial task change.
- `project-management`: Senior management of task lifecycles and assigned owners.
- `structured-system-design`: SSD logic to organize complex product hierarchies.

# 🛠️ Product Owner | Tools
>
> **Scope:** Planning, governance, and user-proxy capabilities.

## 📡 Senses (MCP Protocol)
- **Vision Reader:** Access to initial user prompts and mission statements.
- **Status Sense:** Real-time visibility of the `$TARGET_PROJECT` state via `PROJECT_STATE.json`.
- **Knowledge Bridge:** Read-only access to the global `00_GLOBAL_KNOWLEDGE`.

## 🔧 Internal Tools
- **PRP_Contract_Validator:** Tool to verify the structure and presence of the project contract.
- **Backlog_Manager:** Direct control over task prioritization in `TASKS/01_PENDING/`.
- **Mission_Reporter:** Automated generator of high-level progress reports.
